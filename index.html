🌱 About KrishiChain

Revolutionizing agricultural supply chains with blockchain technology

Our Mission

KrishiChain is dedicated to bringing transparency, traceability, and trust to agricultural supply chains. By leveraging blockchain technology, we connect farmers, distributors, retailers, and consumers in a seamless network that ensures authentic, high-quality produce reaches every table.

How It Works

🌾

Farm Registration

Farmers register their produce with harvest details, creating a unique blockchain record with QR code identification.

🚛

Distribution Tracking

Distributors scan farmer QR codes and add their handling information, maintaining the chain of custody.

🏪

Retail Management

Retailers update final pricing and location information before products reach consumers.

👤

Consumer Verification

Customers can verify product authenticity and view the complete journey from farm to table.

🛡️ Trust & Transparency
